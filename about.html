    • Welcome to my Fashion Haven!

    My name is William Na, the curator and creator behind this fashion blog, where I fuse my love for high fashion with my passion for web development. As an avid enthusiast of avant-garde designers like Rick Owens, Yohji Yamamoto, and Maison Margiela, this platform serves as my canvas to explore, celebrate, and dissect the intricate world of couture. I believe that fashion is more than clothing; it's an art form that transcends boundaries and speaks to individual expression and creativity.

    Driven by my fascination with high fashion, I embarked on the journey of creating this blog as part of my IDM 221 Web Development course. The world of web development provides me with the perfect medium to experiment with code, design, and functionality, allowing me to blend my technical skills with my personal interests seamlessly. This blog is a manifestation of my dedication to both the art of fashion and the art of coding. Here, I share insights into the avant-garde visions of Owens, Yamamoto, and Margiela, unraveling the stories behind each collection and exploring the intersection of technology and couture. Join me on this exciting journey where fashion and coding converge to create a unique space for both exploration and expression.
